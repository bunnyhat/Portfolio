About Me

Hi there! You have come across the portfolio of Zeeshan Mohammed. A game programmer, collector of fan art, tech wiz, redditor and Skyrim enthusiast. Here you can view my résumé, my professional work, work I have done in college, as well as some of my personal fun and interesting projects and 3D models. Currently I am researching and developing tools and mechanics to sharpen my skills as a Game Programmer. Until recently I was an intern then later a full-time coder at Yellow Bear Studios prototyping and making awesome web and mobile games for the younger generations to come. I started on a solo prototype for a kids app showing funny and cute animal memes and pictures. Prior to that, I was a former student and gruadate from triOS College with a degree in Game Design and Development. I specialize in gameplay programming as well as UI programming, but love to tackle any problem I come across.

Where to find me

I would love to chat and discuss more about job opportunities, my experience and qualities, projects I’ve worked on, building a PC, cat pictures, or anything that’s on your mind.
