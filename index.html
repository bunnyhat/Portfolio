Rougelike - Made with Unity

A 4-player 2D dungeon roguelike game, where each player must traverse through randomly generated rooms. Each player has a specific type of skill-set. Players can chose to be a Warrior, Mage, Archer or a Cleric. Fight off skeleton minions and wild beasts together to stay alive and get deeper in the dungeon.

Four Tanks trapped on a map. Assorted power-up scattered around the map. Only one tank can come out alive. Inspired by the retro Bomberman like game but with tanks.

Bounce - Made with Unity
